About TAP

Student Enrollment Report

Introduction

TAP Learning Center, located at 4/42, Jalan Assunta, Petaling Jaya, provides educational support to children, the majority of whom are Myanmar refugees. This report outlines the current student enrollment and class distribution within the center.

Total Enrollment

The total number of students enrolled at TAP Learning Center is 43.

Student Background

Most of the students attending the center are refugees from Myanmar, reflecting the school’s commitment to supporting underserved and vulnerable communities through education.

Class Structure and Distribution

The school operates six classes, ranging from Primary 1 to Primary 6. The distribution of students across these classes is as follows:

  • Primary I : 7 students
  • Primary II : 7 students
  • Primary III : 7 students
  • Primary IV : 9 students
  • Primary V : 3 students
  • Primary VI : 1 students
